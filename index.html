Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish. It features succulent pieces of chicken simmered in a rich, buttery, and creamy tomato-based sauce. The dish is flavored with a blend of aromatic spices, including garam masala, turmeric, and cumin. Serve it with naan or basmati rice for a complete meal.

Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a rich and creamy pasta dish originating from Rome. The dish is traditionally made with eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, guanciale (or pancetta), and freshly ground black pepper. It's a quick and simple recipe, yet delivers a burst of flavor in every bite.

Vegan Buddha Bowl

A nourishing and colorful bowl of quinoa, roasted veggies, and a creamy tahini dressing. This vegan buddha bowl is packed with nutrients and flavor, making it a perfect meal for any time of the day.

Chocolate Lava Cake

Chocolate Lava Cake is a decadent dessert with a gooey chocolate center that flows out when you cut into it. Serve it warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an indulgent treat.
